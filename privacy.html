Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

ISG Generator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to ISG Generator, we collect:

  • TikTok user ID (open_id) and display name
  • Profile avatar (for display purposes only)
  • OAuth access tokens necessary to publish content on your behalf

We do not collect your TikTok password or any sensitive personal data beyond what is required to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Authenticate you via TikTok Login Kit
  • Publish AI-generated content to your TikTok account upon your explicit request
  • Display your profile information within the ISG Generator dashboard

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. TikTok API Data Usage

ISG Generator uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish content on your behalf. We only publish content when you explicitly initiate an action within our platform. We do not access your TikTok feed, followers, messages, or any data beyond what is required for content publishing.

4. Data Storage

OAuth access tokens are stored securely on our servers and are used only to perform actions you explicitly authorize. Tokens are never shared with third parties and are deleted upon account disconnection.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by disconnecting your TikTok account from the platform.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke TikTok account access at any time via TikTok's app settings

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@isggenerator.com
